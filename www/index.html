How to play

  • Pick a chip value, then tap a spot on the table to place it — or drag a chip straight onto a bet.
  • Payouts: a single number pays 35:1; red/black, odd/even, 1–18/19–36 pay 1:1; dozens & columns pay 2:1.
  • You get 30 seconds each normal round and 10 seconds while Auto Bet is active. The wheel auto-spins when the timer reaches zero.
  • Manage bets with Double, Undo, Clear and Repeat. Auto Bet repeats the complete previous betting layout and total amounts each round. Reset restores the full starter profile.
  • The pocket the ball lands in is the winning number — that decides every payout, the history and the stats.
  • Play chips only — no real money, deposits, withdrawals or cash value.

Gold chips and diamonds are virtual items with no cash value.
